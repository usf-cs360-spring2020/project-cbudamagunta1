Rate of Inventors Based on Parental Income

This basic prototype uses the Origins of Inventors: Innovation Rates by Childhood State, Gender, and Parent Income dataset to look at the different share of children who become inventors based on the income quintile of their parents.

Columns Used:

  • par_stateabbrv (State): Two-letter state abbreviation.
  • inventor_pq_1: Share of children who go on to become inventors, restricting the sample to children whose parental income is in quintile 1 of the parent income distribution of the children’s birth cohort.
  • inventor_pq_5: Share of children who go on to become inventors, restricting the sample to children whose parental income is in quintile 5 of the parent income distribution of the children’s birth cohort.
  • top5cit: Share of children with patent citations in top 5% of their birth cohort, using total number of citations.
