Narrative

They say innovation is what drives us forward, but not everyone gets the chance at becoming an inventor. Often this is simply because of the situations they happen to be born into. Looking at the patterns found amongst those who do go on to be inventors can help us understand how to support those voices that do not get heard. Having greater diversity and different perspectives in innovation will lead to greater advancements overall.

Dataset

The Who Becomes an Inventor in America? The Importance of Exposure to Innovation project from Opportunity Insights looks at the key factors amongst individuals who become inventors in the United States, as measured by filing a patent. They look at different conditions that encouraged innovation in these individuals, in order to determine what policies would be most effective at sparking that innovation in other, underrepresented populations.
