My name is Chay, I am a computer science major and a theater minor, and I love finding ways to blend tech and art.

I chose this specific dataset because I found it very intesting to look closer at what conditions are most conducive for innovation across the US. I am personally passionate about allowing for diverse and underrepresented voices to be heard in spaces where they are systematically pushed out. Innovation is something that is supposed to drive us forward, but when that is not accessible to all groups of people, we only get advancement in one specific direction, from one perspective. Looking at datasets like this can help us understand where the system is lacking, and hopefully can encourage change to allow for more opportunity.
