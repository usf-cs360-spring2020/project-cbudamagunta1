Innovation Rates based on Parent Quintile, Gender, and Childhood State of Residence

Purpose

This choropleth maps looks at the specific distribution of innovation rates for different states based on the selected variable, providing a closer look at how these rates change dependant on different factors. The additional information also allows for comparison between all factors to see which contribute to the highest rates. This is an exploratory visualization to further look into what most affects one's likelihood of becoming an inventor, to therefore understand what support needs to be provided to those who do not have those advantages.

Encoding

Encoding: Explain to general users how your data is encoded and how to interpret your visualization(s).

Quantile scale coloring - broken up into categories

Color encodes which bracket of values that state's values fall under for the selected variable. Darker colors mean higher values.

Dataset Used:

Origins of Inventors: Innovation Rates by Childhood State, Gender, and Parent Income

Columns:

  • inventor: share of children overall who go on to become inventors
  • inventor_g_m: same as inventor but restricted to males
  • inventor_g_f: same as inventor but restricted to females
  • inventor_pq_1:same as inventor but restricted to children whose parental income is in quintile 1 of the parent income distribution of the children's birth cohort
  • inventor_pq_2:same as inventor but restricted to children whose parental income is in quintile 2 of the parent income distribution of the children's birth cohort
  • inventor_pq_3:same as inventor but restricted to children whose parental income is in quintile 3 of the parent income distribution of the children's birth cohort
  • inventor_pq_4:same as inventor but restricted to children whose parental income is in quintile 4 of the parent income distribution of the children's birth cohort
  • inventor_pq_5:same as inventor but restricted to children whose parental income is in quintile 5 of the parent income distribution of the children's birth cohort

Interactivity

Brushing: Hovering over a cell on the legend will highlight all states that fall under that category.

Highlighting when hovering over a state.

Details-on-Demand: Hovering over a state will display information about that state.

Filtering: Choose the shown dataset using the dropdown at the top.
